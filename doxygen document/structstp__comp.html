◆ knee_width

float stp_comp::knee_width

Important: It only accepts 0 or 1 as input. 1 Sets soft knee (20dB) and 0 hard knee (0dB). It controls whether the bend in the compression characteristic (see Fig. 1) has a sharp angle or has a rounded edge.

◆ makeup_gain

float stp_comp::makeup_gain

in dB. The compressor reduces the level (gain) of the signal; therefore, applying a make-up gain to the signal allows for matching the input and output loudness level.
